How CoderDojo Works

CoderDojo is a volunteer-led group of programming schools, essentially.

Membership is free of charge, and a dojo could be located anywhere from a school to a person's home.

Everyone can work at their own pace, in their own preferred programming language, and request help from others attending.

Each dojo acts independently of each other.

Age Categories

Our club has two age categories. People aged from 7 - 16 can attend the club.
