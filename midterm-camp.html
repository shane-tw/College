Midterm Camp

In October 2015, the CoderDojo Club will be holding its annual coding Summer camp.

This is a wonderful opportunity for kids between the ages of 7 and 11 to learn about the world of coding.

Kids will learn, throughout the duration of the camp, how to effectively develop their own games.

Dates: October 27th - 29th
Time: 10:00 am to 03:00 pm
